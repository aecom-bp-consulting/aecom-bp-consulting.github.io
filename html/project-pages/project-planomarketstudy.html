Plano Market Study

City of Plano, TX

The City of Plano, TX, contracted AECOM to develop a set of public-facing dashboards that described the current status as well as historical data surrounding demographics, housing, and commercial real estate. To create these dashboards, AECOM leveraged data from multiple sources – the American Community Survey, Bureau of Labor Statistics, Lightcast.io, CoStar, and City of Plano data. The dashboards were created using ESRI software, including the ArcGIS API for Python, Experience Builder, and ArcGIS HubSite. These dashboards analysed metrics at a geospatial level, using Plano’s neighbourhoods as a geographic unit, or through point data. Across the three dashboards, this study consisted of over 50 unique, interactive maps.

For the demographics dashboard, in-house Python scripts were developed to connect to the Census Bureau’s API and pull and analyse data on metrics such as population, median income, race and ethnicity, education and owner and renter statistics. Historical data from 2010 was compared to the most recent year of data, and using the ArcGIS API for Python, the mapping process was automated. This dashboard allows users to identify changes in time across the City of Plano.

The housing dashboard was primarily concerned with the current state of housing in the City – availability, pricing, and conditions. Using American Community Survey data as well as in-house City of Plano data, this dashboard shed light on several advanced metrics such as the susceptibility to change, investor-confidence ratio, and Area-Median-Income (AMI) tiers. This dashboard also leveraged Census LODES data to map commuting sources and destinations for residents across the Dallas metro area, supplemented by an insight into Jobs-Housing balances for the constituent cities.

The commercial real estate dashboard (also pictured above) analysed growth in employment, growth in commercial space over time, commercial susceptibility to change, recent additions and renovations, and change in commercial property valuation over time. This dashboard leveraged data from Lightcast.io, CoStar, and the City of Plano to provide a clearer picture of the past and present of commercial real estate across the City.

Finally, AECOM has also been contracted to annually update these dashboards with data from the most recent year available.
