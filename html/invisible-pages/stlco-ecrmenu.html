Economic Development Look into St. Louis County's economic status in terms of employment, worker composition, and real estate. Housing and People Evaluate St. Louis County's housing supply, diversity, and affordability, as well as looking into the County's household characterisitcs. Transportation and Infrastructure Look at the County's roadway, transit, bicycle, pedestrian, freight network, and utilities. Open Space and Environment Examine how the County's natural systems impact residents' quality of life. Land Use and Development An examination of the County's land use patterns and their impact on environmental justice.
